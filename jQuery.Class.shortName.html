shortName

  • property
 

The name of the class without its namespace, provided for introspection purposes.

$.Class("MyOrg.MyClass",{},{})
MyOrg.MyClass.shortName //-> 'MyClass'
MyOrg.MyClass.fullName //->  'MyOrg.MyClass'
